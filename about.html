About Me

Hello! I’m an artist passionate about capturing emotions, nature, and abstract ideas through my work. My journey has been shaped by exhibitions, collections, and recognitions over the years. I believe art is a universal language, and through my creations, I aim to evoke feelings, inspire thought, and create a connection with the audience.

Education

  • 🎓 BFA in Fine Arts, SRM University
  • 🎨 MFA in Painting, Delhi University

My education helped me develop a solid foundation in artistic techniques and theories, enabling me to explore and innovate freely.

Awards & Recognition

  • 🏆 National Art Award, 2023
  • 🌟 Best Contemporary Artist, 2022

These recognitions motivate me to push boundaries and explore new dimensions in my art.

Exhibitions

  • 🏛 Modern Art Gallery, Mumbai (2024)
  • 🌐 International Expo, London (2023)

Showcasing my work internationally has allowed me to connect with art lovers from different cultures and perspectives.

Collections

My works are part of private collections in India, UK, and the US. Each piece reflects a personal story and resonates with the emotions of the viewers.

Art is not just a profession for me—it is a journey of self-expression, creativity, and connection. Every brushstroke tells a story, and every color evokes a feeling.
